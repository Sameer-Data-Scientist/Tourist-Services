                                                   About Program


          This program is an all-in-one travel assistant. It features a voice translator for easy communication,
           a historical place finder to explore landmarks, a guidebook to help plan visits, and an accommodation
           or hotel finder for a comfortable stay. With powerful Google libraries integrated, it ensures smooth
           and efficient functionality, making it perfect for travelers seeking convenience and information
           in one place.
